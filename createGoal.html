What is a S.M.A.R.T Goal?

  • Specific: A specific goal has a much greater chance of being accomplished than a general goal.
  • Measurable: Establish concrete criteria for measuring progress toward the attainment of the goal.
  • Attainable: When a goal is attainable, it is realistic and can be achieved.
  • Relevant: The goal is relevant to the overall mission and purpose of the organization.
  • Time-bound: Every goal should have a deadline, otherwise, it is just a dream.
